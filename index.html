    The Pyrénées are a range of mountain in the south of France. Natural border between this country and Spain, it extends for about 491km from the Atlantic ocean to the Mediterranean sea. Its max height is 11168fts (3404m) at the peak of Aneto.

    This is the Vallée d'Ossau, hence my nickname. This is a beautiful protected space where i often go to hike. This is an easy and peaceful place, with numerous breathtaking views. From a couple hours to several days, you can hike as long as you want here, there is a refuge named Ayous where you can sleep, but not for free.
    Refuge d'Ayous

    The climate is temperate. Around -10°c in a cold winter to 35°c in summer and higher is colder, it's important to have the right equipment if you don't want to die of hypothermia or hyperthermia. Happily, France is a huge manufacturer of good sleepings bags, like Triple Zero.
